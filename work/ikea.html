Supply chain platform for IKEA

Real-time visibility for the raw materials that fuel IKEA's global operations — timber and plants moving across continents, tracked without spreadsheets.

UI/UX Designer (solo) 4 months Supply chain · B2B 8-person Agile squad

The challenge

Slow, disconnected tools — visibility scattered across spreadsheets and email threads.

Stakeholder and vendor interviews surfaced the core pain points. An audit revealed tracking gaps, fragmented alerting and manual reporting burdens.

The brief: one internal platform where teams and vendors trace materials in real time — built to WCAG accessibility standards.

The work

Research to wireframes

User journeys mapped with IKEA staff and vendors — fast iteration from low-fidelity wireframes to high-fidelity UI inside an 8-person Agile team.

Tracing every event

Real-time dashboard, facility management, automated alerts and full transaction history — any material traceable from forest to facility, with one-click compliance reports.

The outcomes

0moDiscovery → delivery
LiveMaterial traceability
1-clickCompliance reports
WCAGAccessible by design
  • Manual spreadsheet reporting replaced with automated, scheduled reports.
  • Scattered email alerts consolidated into one configurable alert centre.
  • Vendor satisfaction and adoption increased across stakeholders.
  • Usability sessions with staff and vendors directly refined final workflows.
