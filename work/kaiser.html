Work · 04 of 05 · 2023

POS portal for Kaiser Permanente

One centralized portal to manage Oracle Xstore POS systems across 39 hospitals and 700+ medical stores — where downtime directly affects care.

UI/UX Designer (solo) 4 months Healthcare · Enterprise Research → handoff

The challenge

Admins reading raw logs by hand — while every minute of downtime impacts hospital operations.

No centralized oversight of performance, user access or log data existed. Manual processes meant slow incident response and a heightened risk of configuration errors.

I shadowed IT leads, system admins and operations managers to map real workflows — log review, access changes, incident response — before drawing a single screen.

The work

Dashboard-centric design

Every POS location, monitored from one screen. Critical information surfaces upfront; workflows stay action-oriented — built to minimize cognitive load during incidents.

Validated with admins

Stakeholder walkthroughs with working prototypes refined the interaction patterns against real admin feedback — through sprint ceremonies and UAT until the build matched the design.

The outcomes
