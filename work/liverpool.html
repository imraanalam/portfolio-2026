Mobile POS for Liverpool

Putting the whole store in every sales rep's hand — orders, product info and personal performance, all from a single mobile interface.

UI/UX Designer (solo) 2 months Retail · B2B Mobile POS

The challenge

Long order workflows, sluggish lookup, zero performance visibility — friction that cost sales.

I interviewed in-store sales staff and observed daily routines to find the real bottlenecks: slow product search, confusing payment flows and limited customer visibility.

Liverpool needed reps equipped to close sales on the spot — anywhere on the floor.

The work

Speed & clarity first

Smart product search, streamlined checkout, customer lookup and a real-time performance dashboard — every screen designed for a busy shop floor.

Piloted on the floor

In-store pilot testing with real staff shaped the final navigation and UI. I owned the end-to-end process — concept to annotated handoff — across distributed teams in Agile sprints.

Thoughtful micro-interactions boost rep confidence — and POS design must prioritize speed above all.

The outcomes

0moConcept → handoff
↑Checkout velocity
↓Order entry errors
LivePerformance dashboards
  • Enhanced in-store efficiency and faster checkout.
  • Reduced order entry errors across reps.
  • Improved conversion via faster customer support on the floor.
  • Positive staff feedback on clarity from day one of the pilot.
