Work · 03 of 05 · 2024

eCommerce app for Petsworld

Meeting busy pet parents where they are — on their phones, between walks, feedings and coffee runs. Simple, personal, stress-free shopping.

UI/UX Designer 4 months eCommerce · Startup iOS & Android

The challenge

Not more sales — a shopping experience that respects how little time pet parents have.

Surveys and interviews with pet owners, plus a competitor audit, exposed the gaps: slow browsing, clumsy reordering and zero visibility once an order shipped.

Speed, personalization and trust became the three pillars behind every flow.

The work

Journeys first

Critical journeys mapped — rapid reordering, order tracking, discovery — then flows and wireframes in Figma, built for one-handed use.

One-tap everything

Fast browsing, one-tap reordering, personalized suggestions, secure streamlined checkout, real-time order tracking — every feature shaving seconds off everyday tasks.

The outcomes

↑Mobile conversions
↑Repeat orders

0moTo launch
  • Significantly increased mobile conversions after launch.
  • Higher repeat-order rates driven by the one-tap reorder flow.
