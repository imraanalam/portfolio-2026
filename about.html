About — turning ideas into reality since 2015

Designer. Systems thinker. Problem chooser.

Hyderabad, India · —

I believe the interface should disappear.

For over a decade I've designed enterprise, B2B and SaaS products across PLM, supply chain, logistics, healthcare and eCommerce — leading design from discovery and research through prototyping, validation and delivery.

Right now I'm Senior UI/UX Designer at Z2Data, leading UX for a supply-chain risk and compliance platform used by 150+ global enterprises — including RAG workflows, AI agents and intelligent data exploration tools.

Before that: rebuilding Shipeezi's logistics platform and multi-brand design system, five enterprise engagements at Skillnet (IKEA, Labcorp, ATD, Liverpool, Kaiser Permanente), and seven years running my own studio — 150+ websites and 18,000+ template copies sold on Envato.

I also hold a Master's in Psychology and I'm pursuing a UI/UX postgrad at IIT Roorkee — which explains the obsession with how people actually think.

How I think

Five operating principles.

01

User-centred design

Every decision traces back to a real user need, validated through research — not assumption.

02

Systems thinking

Products are ecosystems. Components, patterns and flows designed to scale together.

03

Cognitive psychology

Reducing cognitive load is a feature. A psychology degree shapes how I structure information.

04

Emotional design

Enterprise software doesn't have to feel like a tax form. Craft and micro-interactions build trust.

05

Data-informed decisions

Qualitative insight plus quantitative evidence — A/B tests, analytics and usability metrics close the loop.
